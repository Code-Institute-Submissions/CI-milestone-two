About us

Our goal

Ever gotten so bored of trying to find something good to watch that you've fallen asleep on the couch? We have... We want to help you wade through the masses of viewing options available to you on tv, streaming and eh... other more nefarious means which we could not possibly condone, by providing you lightning quick access to review scores and other handy info without all the nonsense that goes along with using our more established competitors.


Stick around and let us know what you think in the chat forum. If you have time that is, we don't want to interrupt your viewing schedule :)
